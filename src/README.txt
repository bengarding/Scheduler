# Scheduler

Scheduler is an application that stores contacts and appointments into a database. Users can add, edit, and delete customers and appointments.
Users can also generate reports to view counts of appointments by month and also by type of appointment.
You can view appointments for each contact and for each customer.

## Directions

To use this application, first log in and when you reach the main page, you can switch between the tabs to show either
appointments or contacts in the database. From the menu bar, you can click 'New' to create a new appointment or contact.

From the 'Reports' menu, you can view counts of appointments by month or appointment type. You can also view appointments
